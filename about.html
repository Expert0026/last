About Us

Marshall Speakers, the iconic brand that has become synonymous with quality sound, has a rich history that dates back to the 1960s. Founded by Jim Marshall, who could be very well called The Father of Modern Sound was a self-taught engineer who had passion of music.


About Jim: Born on 29 July 1923 in London, he spent much of his childhood in hospital receiving treatment for Bone TB. His dad encouraged him to take up dancing to strengthen his legs and this is where his love for music started Jim got his first break to become a drummer after the original drummer of the 16-member band got enlisted for WWII. He quickly became successful in his own right, not only as a drummer but also as a teacher. Some of his most notable students included Mitch Mitchell (The Jimi Hendrix Experience) and Micky Waller, best known for his work with the Jeff Beck Group which featured Rod Stewart on vocals. In early 60s, he began selling musical instruments and amplifiers from his newly-opened music shop in London. However, he soon realised that the amplifiers available at that time were not up to par and musicians were struggling to get the sound they wanted. Jim immediately saw an opportunity to change that and started tinkering with amplifiers to improve their sound quality.


Unique Qualities That Make Marshall Speakers Stand Out:
Iconic Design - One of the most notable design features of Marshall speakers is their use of the "basketweave" pattern on the speaker cabinet. This iconic design is inspired by the original Marshall amplifiers of the 1960s and has become synonymous with the brand. The basketweave pattern not only looks great, but it also provides extra durability and protection for the speaker components.
Sound Engineering - Marshall speakers are designed to produce a classic rock and roll tone, with a warm and rich sound that emphasises mid-range frequencies. Marshall engineers use a combination of modern and vintage technologies to achieve their distinctive sound through a combination of high-quality components, including specially designed speakers, custom-built cabinets, and unique circuitry.
Durability - Marshall speaker were designed to withstand the rigours of touring and heavy use and the philosophy extend in their designs even today. The cabinets are made of high-quality wood, which provides excellent acoustic properties and is highly resistant to wear and tear. Additionally, the cabinets are reinforced with metal corner protectors, which help to protect the speakers during transport and while on stage.

Marshall Amplification

Founded in 1962 by Jim Marshall, Marshall Amplification is a renowned British company specializing in the manufacturing of guitar amplifiers. With a rich history and a legacy deeply rooted in the world of music, Marshall has played a significant role in shaping the sound of rock and other music genres.
